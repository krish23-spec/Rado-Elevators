CERTIFICATES

Our Certifications

ISO 9001:2015

Quality Management System certification — ensuring consistent, documented quality across all our processes and installations.

BIS Certification

Bureau of Indian Standards certification confirming our products and processes meet national standards and specifications.

IS 14665 Compliance

Full compliance with Indian Standards IS 14665 for electric traction lifts — the baseline safety standard for Indian elevators.

Government Approved

Registered and approved by state lift departments across all states in which we operate, with valid installation licenses.

Fire Safety Compliance

All installations meet the National Building Code (NBC) fire safety provisions including fireman's lift requirements.

Technician Certification

All our engineers hold manufacturer and government-issued certifications, renewed annually through structured training.

COMPLIANCE

Standards We Follow

IS 14665 — Indian Elevator Standards

All passenger and goods elevators comply with the mandatory Indian Standards for safety, dimensions, and loading.

Annual Safety Audits by Certified Inspectors

Every elevator in our AMC network undergoes a documented annual inspection by third-party certified lift inspectors.

Manufacturer-Certified Technicians

Our engineers are trained and certified by original equipment manufacturers ensuring genuine expertise, not just experience.

ARD — Automatic Rescue Device

All installed elevators include ARD systems that automatically bring the elevator to the nearest floor during power failure.

Overload Sensors & Door Safety

Every elevator is fitted with calibrated overload protection sensors and infrared door safety edges as standard.
